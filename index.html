About Me

Who's
Tiger?

Tiger Vroman is a 16-year-old student, sales rep, and aspiring entrepreneur based in Austin, TX. A sophomore at Austin Waldorf School, he approaches everything he does with genuine curiosity and an unusual level of self-awareness for his age.

Inspired by his father and a circle of successful mentors, Tiger stepped into the world of sales during his spring break — and didn't look back. Fluent in Russian and deeply passionate about music, fashion, and building real connections, he moves through the world with intention.
